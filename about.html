AKA Scrawlon

What is a Scrawlon? Is it a slang? Does it mean something rude???? No. No. No. It was originally the name of long defunct web application I created -- kind of a cross between Twitter and Evernote, if you can imagine. Anyway, I always liked the sound of it. It's short and memorable, like a fictional computer hacker (Neo, Count Zero, Mr. Robot). Scrawlon has been my Internet moniker for many years now.
