Scrawlon Is...

...the home of web developer, Scott McGrath (ME!). Read the Blog for programming tutorials and tech-related posts. Check my Portfolio for examples and detailed writeups of recent web projects.

The name "Scrawlon" came from one of my first web projects, a phone-based note-taking app from the pre-smartphone era. The idea was to text myself short notes that could be searched later on - kind of a cross between Twitter and Evernote. The app never fully materialized, but I liked the name so much that I've taken it as my online moniker.
